Max Temperature

Plotting maximum temperature against latitude shows that cities closer to the equator (latitude = zero) tend to be hotter than cities further away from the equator. We can also see that cities in the southern hemisphere (negative latitudes) have higher max temperatures at this particular time of year, and that there seem to be fewer cities in the dataset that are situated really far south than really far north.
